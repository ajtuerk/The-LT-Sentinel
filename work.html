At the Newsdesk, I help provide NPR's website with digital stories of weekend breaking news. The topics range wildly, as can be expected, but it has given me a wealth of knowledge both of international situations and politics, but also experience in covering a story fully and correctly using only wire services and whomever I can get a hold of on a Saturday (hint: it's not many!).

During the summer of 2019, I worked at The Sentinel Papers, a local gazette that covered both Prince George's County and Montgomery County. My first journalism internship, it was a great way to both hone the basic skills of reporting a story and put together a clip packet that helped get me to my current position at NPR.
